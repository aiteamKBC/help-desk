2. Summary

KSBs are parts of the learning plan in Aptem, and follow the apprenticeships' standards established by the British government. By visiting "Skills England" you can find our programs' standards and assessment/apprenticeship plan, which includes KSBs. Note: Our programs are Marketing Manager, Marketing Executive, Project Control Manager

3. Steps

1. Open https://skillsengland.education.gov.uk/apprenticeships/?routes=sales-marketing-and-procurement 2. In search box, type program name. 3. Open corresponding program. 4. Find assessment plan at the right side. 5. Download plan.
