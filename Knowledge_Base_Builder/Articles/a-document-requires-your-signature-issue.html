2. Summary

Learner was not able to sign the learning plan, after getting an e-mail to perform the action, this can be solved usually by going to the programme wizard, then finish the process (go to last step, and press finish), the apprenticeship agreement will be shown to the learner so they can sign it, or save it if it already signed, after that the training plan should be accessible.

Attachments

No attachments added.

3. Steps

1. Open Aptem and sign in 2. Choose a laerner 3. Select "learner profile" under learner's name 4. Select "open in classic mode" 5. Go to wizard under "Programme" section 6. Go to the last step 7. Press "Finish" 8. Sign the document if already not.
